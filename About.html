About Us

Indian Railways is the backbone of the country's logistics sector. We carry more than 1.2 Billon tonnes of freight traffic every year over a network of 68000 kms. With our network touching almost every nook and corner of the country, we play a crucial role in facilitating a balanced and inclusive socio economic development of the country. We carry almost all commodities including bulk commodities like Coal, Iron ore, Iron & Steel, Food grains, Cement, Petroleum products, Fertilizer and other commodities carried in containers.
